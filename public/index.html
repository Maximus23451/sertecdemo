Project Overview

Hi! And welcome to our project page; this is MHD. This project was created for our finals. MHD is a medium-sized (fictional) company with three offices spread throughout the city of Budapest and is a cybersecurity company. We have a vast array of devices, VLANs, WLANs, and much more.

To name the offices:

The Right Office, which was configured by Kurucz Zoltán

The Middle Office, which was configured by Me Szabó Márton

And last, but not least, the left office was configured by Zubán Máté.

Each office is equipped with state-of-the-art technology designed to enhance our cybersecurity measures and ensure the safety of our clients data. Our team, composed of highly skilled professionals, is dedicated to staying ahead of emerging threats in the digital landscape.
